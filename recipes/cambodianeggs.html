Cambodian Egg

Description

Cambodian fried eggs gives you a taste of salty with a hint of sweetness, it's simple yet tasty and fulfilling.
It only takes 15 minutes and 8 ingredients to make.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 green onion
  • 1 tsp soysauce
  • 1 tsp fish sauce
  • 1 tsp of water
  • 3 pinch of sugar
  • 1 pinch black pepper
  • 3 tbsp oil

Steps

  1. Chop green onions to 1/4 inch thick
  2. Crack eggs into a bowl
  3. Mix green onion with the egg
  4. Mix in water,soysauce, fish sauce sugar and black pepper in the egg bowl
  5. Heat pan with oil
  6. Once the pan is hot, pour in egg mixture. Cook a couple seconds on both side.

Serve with white rice (optional). We like to eat it with plain jasmine rice
